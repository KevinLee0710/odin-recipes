Peri Peri Chicken Rice

Description

This dish is a delicious and high protein meal you can add to your meal prep repetoir to say goodbye to boring chicken rice and brocoli. It is easy and quick to prepare and will make your cuts a little less miserable.

Macros

Macros Per Serving

Ingredients (4 servings)

Peri-peri Chicken

  • Chicken thigh, boneless, skinless, excess fat removed - 800g
  • Nando's Peri-peri sauce - 100g
  • Salt - 1tsp
  • Smoked paprika - 1tsp
  • Chilli powder - 1tsp
  • Garlic powder - 1tsp
  • Onion powder - 1tsp
  • Cumin - 1tsp
  • Oregano - 1tsp
  • Lemon juice - 20ml
  • Light butter (for cooking)(unnecessary if cooking chicken in the oven) - 20g

Light Perinaise Sauce

  • Light mayo - 100g
  • Nando's Peri-peri sauce - 80g
  • Garlic powder - 1tsp
  • Smoked paprika - 1tsp

Rice

  • Rice, uncooked - 300g
  • Garlic, minced - 3 cloves
  • Onion, diced - 1 medium
  • Bell pepper, diced - 1
  • Tomato paste - 50g
  • Salt - 1tsp
  • Smoked paprika - 1tsp
  • Chilli powder - 1tsp
  • Cumin - 1tsp
  • Turmuric - 1tsp

Instructions

Prep

  1. Combine spices, seasonings, and peri-peri sauce listed under "Peri-Peri Chicken" and marinate for at least 30min, up to over night.
  2. If cooking chicken with the oven, preheat on 350F/175C. If not then ignore this step.
  3. Dice onion, and bell pepper.
  4. Mince garlic.
  5. Cook your rice (for best results use a rice cooker).
  6. Combine all ingredients listed under "Light Perinaise Sauce". Bottle and refrigerate for future use.

Cooking

  1. If cooking chicken in the oven,set the oven to broil and cook the chicken until the edges start to char. Flip, set the oven to 350, and cook until it reaches your prefered temperature.
  2. If cooking chicken in a pan, pre-heat a pan on medium-high and then cook the chicken in light butter until it has become golden-brown and has reached your prefered temperature.
  3. Once your chicken has cooked, chop into bite sized cubes, portion, and set it aside.
  4. Take the diced onion, and bell pepper and saute until the onion is transluscent.
  5. Add the minced garlic to the pan and continue to saute until fragrant.
  6. Add the spices, seasonings, and tomato paste listed under "Rice" to the pan and mix to combine well.
  7. Add the contents of the pan to your cooked rice and mix until combined, and then portion along-side the chicken that has been set aside.
  8. Top with the pre-mixed perinaise sauce and serve.
